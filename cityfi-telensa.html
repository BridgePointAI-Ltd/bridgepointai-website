The Starting Point

Telensa Has Already Done the Hard Part.

Fifteen years of deployment. 2.1 million connected control nodes. PLANet — the world's most trusted lighting management platform. Urban IQ — the sensor connectivity layer that proved streetlight poles are the smart city infrastructure backbone. Signify's global distribution.

Telensa is not a streetlight company that dabbles in smart cities. It is the smart city infrastructure company that happens to control the lights. That distinction matters — because it defines what the next chapter looks like.

The Opportunity

Urban IQ Opened the Door. CityFi Walks Through It.

Urban IQ connected the principle: streetlight poles are the natural host for city-wide sensing. It brought in air quality, environmental, waste and road sensors from multiple third-party vendors. The platform works. The concept is proven.

The friction is vendor complexity. Every sensor type requires a separate provider, separate hardware, separate integration, separate commercial relationship. CityFi solves that in a single device.

The CityFi LuxMetrix Device

One Retrofit. Seventeen Capabilities.

LuxMetrix mounts directly onto existing streetlight infrastructure — NEMA and Zhaga standard housings — with no pole replacement, no new cable runs, and no civil engineering. It activates immediately on existing wireless networks.
