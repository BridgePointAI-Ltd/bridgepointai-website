The Opportunity

The prize isn't diagnosis — it's the patient relationship.

For an acquirer, the near-term value is owning the patient channel, generating adherence & outcomes data, and reducing nurse workload. All of that can be captured before taking on the regulatory burden of a diagnostic device.

Option 1 — Launch Now

A monitoring & communication platform.

  • Patient diary: symptoms, leakage, quality-of-life
  • Image & video capture over time
  • Trend reporting — the one new build, no AI needed
  • Nurse messaging & peer-support community
  • Lowest regulatory burden · fastest UK rollout
06 / 11
Option 2 — Follow-On

Switch the clinical engine on.

The consultation flow, the ontology that suggests complications & recommendations, and the clinician info card already exist and have been demoed. Moving up is re-enabling — plus compliance.

  • Re-enable e-consultation & clinical decision support
  • Clinician dashboard, risk scoring, population analytics
  • Device classification, QMS & clinical validation
  • NHS integration & CQC registration
