Why 10,000°C Matters

Conventional gasification operates at 1,200°C. At 10,000°C, molecular bonds that no other process can break — including the C-F bonds in PFAS "forever chemicals" — are fully dissociated. NZP is the only commercially viable pathway for PFAS destruction at scale. No pre-treatment required. Feedstock moisture above 50% accepted.
