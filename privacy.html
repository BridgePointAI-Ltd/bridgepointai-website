We process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) — specifically, to respond to your direct enquiry — and where applicable, with your consent when you voluntarily submit a contact form.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@bridgepointai.io.
